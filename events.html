Carnifex is an American deathcore band from San Diego County, California. Carnifex play an extreme metal musical style known as deathcore, which is a hybrid of the genres death metal and metalcore. Carnifex's lyrics are all based around negative themes. Lead vocalist Scott Lewis has stated the theme of Carnifex will always be negative even when the state of mind he is in is positive.
